About Us

At Tawreed, we understand the importance of seamless logistics and efficient fulfillment services for your business. Our mission is to empower businesses of all sizes by providing top-notch, reliable, and cost-effective fulfillment solutions.

Sort

We take care of sorting products through receiving and delivering them using a streamlined workflow, making it easy to handle when requested.

Storage

We strive to store in designated places according to the nature of the products, and inventory is managed through a specialized and precise program that helps the client monitor inventory and manage their products

Shipping

To ensure your product reaches you in the fastest way possible, we carefully select delivery partners to provide a service that satisfies our customers in terms of both the offered services and coverage network.

return

We understand the diverse preferences of our customers. However, we handle returns with precision and efficiency, reintegrating them into our inventory to derive value in other operations.
